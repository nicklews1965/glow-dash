## Description

Dodge neon obstacles. Beat your score. Unlock skins. How far can you dash?

Glow Dash is a fast-paced, one-tap arcade game set in a stunning neon cyberpunk world. Tap to fly your glowing bird through ever-shifting neon barriers. Every 15 points, a Neon Pulse wave sweeps the screen and shifts the entire color palette — keeping every run fresh and visually exciting.

SIMPLE TO PLAY, HARD TO MASTER
- One-tap controls: tap to fly, release to fall
- Obstacles get faster and gaps get tighter as your score climbs
- Moving obstacles appear at higher scores for an extra challenge

NEON PULSE COLOR SHIFTS
- The color palette shifts every 15 points with a dramatic pulse wave effect
- Cyan, magenta, yellow, green, orange, pink — the world transforms as you play

10 UNLOCKABLE CHARACTER SKINS
- Earn new skins by reaching score milestones and playing games
- 4 unique shapes: Chevron, Orb, Diamond, and Bolt
- Show off your favorites — all earned by playing, never by paying

DAILY CHALLENGES
- A new challenge every day to keep you coming back
- Score targets, game count goals, and total point challenges
- Complete challenges to unlock the rare Prismatic skin

COMPETE GLOBALLY
- Game Center leaderboards — see how you rank worldwide
- 8 achievements to earn from beginner to expert

SHARE YOUR SCORES
- Screenshot your best runs and share to social media
- Challenge friends to beat your high score

DESIGNED TO LOOK AMAZING
- All visuals generated with neon glow effects — no pixel art needed
- 3-layer parallax scrolling with city silhouettes and floating shapes
- Particle trails, score sparkles, and screen-shake juice
- Liquid Glass UI design for a premium, modern feel

100% FREE
- No paywalls, no pay-to-win, no locked content behind purchases
- Everything is earned by playing
- Supported by optional, non-intrusive ads

Download Glow Dash and see how far you can fly!
