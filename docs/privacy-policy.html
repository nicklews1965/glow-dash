Privacy Policy for LitFlap

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Introduction

LitFlap ("the Game", "we", "our") is a free-to-play mobile game. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Game. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

The Game itself does not require an account and does not collect personal information directly. However, the following data may be collected through third-party services:

Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — only with your explicit permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Device type, operating system version, and screen size
  • General location (country/region level, not precise location)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • App usage data for ad relevance

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Apple Game Center, your Game Center display name and scores are shared with Apple's Game Center service according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Locally Stored Data

The Game stores the following data locally on your device only (never transmitted to us):

  • High scores and game statistics
  • Sound and haptic preferences
  • Unlocked skins and selected skin
  • Daily challenge progress

How We Use Information

  • To display advertisements that support the free Game
  • To provide leaderboard and achievement features via Game Center
  • To remember your game preferences and progress

Your Choices

  • Tracking: You can deny the App Tracking Transparency prompt. The Game works fully without tracking — ads will simply be less relevant.
  • Ad Personalization: You can manage ad personalization through the GDPR/privacy consent dialog shown on first launch.
  • Game Center: Game Center sign-in is optional.
  • Local Data: Uninstalling the Game deletes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

The Game is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Ads shown are not targeted based on personal data when tracking permission is not granted.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with:

  • Google AdMob: For ad serving and measurement
  • Apple Game Center: For leaderboards and achievements (if you opt in)

Data Security

Game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Third-party services (Google, Apple) maintain their own security practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. Since we do not collect personal information directly, these rights are exercised through our third-party service providers (Google). You may opt out of personalized advertising via the consent dialog or by denying tracking permission.

EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and objection. The GDPR consent dialog presented on first launch allows you to exercise your right to choose. For data held by Google, refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
